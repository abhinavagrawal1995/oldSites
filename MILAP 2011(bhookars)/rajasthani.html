FAMOUS RAJASTHANI DISHES ARE:-

 

DAL BATTI CHURMA

BHOOKARS RATINGS:-

Rajasthani food is incomplete without the mention of the famed Dal-Baati-Churma. Baatis, irrespective of their cooking technique is always served dipped in ghee accompanied with panchmel or panch kutti dal and churma.

WHERE TO EAT -JAIN DHABA (BIKANER)

 

GATTA KI SABZI

BHOOKARS RATINGS:-

Gatte ki sabji is a popular and all-time favorite Marwari recipe with besan or gram flour based steamed dumplings simmered in buttermilk laden tangy gravy. Enjoy the gatte ki subzi with bajra roti or rice.

WHERE TO EAT:-JAIN DHABA (BIKANER)

 

KERI SANGRI

BHOOKARS RATINGS:-

 Ker Sangri is one of the most mouth-watering delicacies of Jaisalmer.It is a unique combination of berries and dried beans cooked with yogurt and masalas cooked on low heat. This vegetarian delicacy can be best enjoyed with Bajara roti.

WHERE TO EAT:- JAIN DHABA (JAIN RESTAURANT)
