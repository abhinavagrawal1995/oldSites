   1. Basmati Rice – 1 cup
   2. Small onions – 5,6
      Green chillies – 2
      Turmeric powder – 1/2 tsp
      Lemon juice – of 1 lemon
      Curry leaves
      Salt
   3. Mustard – 1 tsp
   4. Sesame oil – 1 tbsp

Add half tablespoon of oil to the rice and cook it the normal way. Heat the remaining oil in a pan and splutter the mustard seeds. Add chopped small onion, curry leaves, green chillies, turmeric powder and salt. Stir for a few minutes. Add the cooked rice and lemon juice and mix well. Cook for two more minutes and remove from gas. Serve hot with raitha or plain curd and pickle.
