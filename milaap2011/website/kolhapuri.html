KOLHAPURI CHICKEN

RECIPE

Ingredients:

500 gm Chicken - cut into pieces
1/3 cup Yogurt
1 tsp Grated coconut
2 chopped Onions
1 chopped Tomato
1 Bay leaf
1 Cinnamon
3 Cloves
1 tsp Peanut oil
1 tsp Red chilly powder
1/2 tsp Garlic paste
1/2 tsp Coriander leaves
1/2 tsp Turmeric powder
1/2 tsp Lime juice
1/4 tsp Crushed black pepper
1 tbsp Oil
Salt to taste

Method:

1.In a bowl, combine the yogurt,turmeric powder, red chillies, garlic paste, salt and lime juice.
2.Marinate the chicken pieces in yogurt mixture for 30-40 minutes..
3.Heat peanut oil in pan, add the bay leaf, cinnamon, cloves, black pepper and onions, saute until onions turn translucent.
4.Add the coconut and saute for a minute.
5.Add the tomatoes and cook for 6-7 minutes until mushy.
6.Cool the ingredients and grind into paste.
7.Heat the oil in a pan, add the marinated chicken and cook for 15-20 minutes on low fire.
8.Add the paste, stir well and simmer for couple of minutes until chicken is well cooked.
9.Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot with rice.
