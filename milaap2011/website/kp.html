KARWARI PRAWNS

RECIPE

Ingredients:

3 tablespoons vegetable oil
1 onion, chopped
1 coconut, drained and grated
1/2 teaspoon ground turmeric
1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1 teaspoon salt
300g tiger prawns, peeled and deveined

Method:

In a large frying pan, heat oil over medium heat.
Add onion and sauté till softened.
Add coconut to the onions and sauté for a few minutes.
Mix in turmeric, cayenne pepper and salt.
Add prawns and stir.
Reduce heat to low and cook for 15 minutes. Serve hot.
