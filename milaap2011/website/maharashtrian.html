FAMOUS MAHARASHTRIAN DISHES ARE:-

 

PAV BHAJI

  BOOKHARS RATINGS:-

Pav bhaji is a hurried meal for the man in the street. This is a spicy blend of vegetables in tomato gravy served with pav or bun bread that is cooked with butter. This is truly an innovation that arose out of the necessity of providing a nice hot meal in a hurry and one that tickles the taste buds.

WHERE TO EAT - STREETS OF MUMBAI.

                                       

KARWARI PRAWNS

BHOOKARS RATINGS:-

Karwari Prawn is a prawn preparation made with coconut. Typical to the coastal areas of Karwar in Maharashtra and Karnataka state.

WHERE TO EAT - GUPTA BROTHERS

 

 

 

KOLHAPURI CHICKEN

BHOOKARS RATINGS-

    Bold, and spicy is what defines this chicken curry; a very aromatic dish that heavily pronounces the flavors from the south western part of India – Kolhapur; a recipe as vibrant and rich as the history of this ancient town. The combination of fresh coconut, onions and tomatoes make a unique masala when blended.

WHERE TO EAT:- AMBAR RESTAURANT
