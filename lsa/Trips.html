Educational Trips

 

Event:

 Trip to Delhi, Mussourie, Rishikesh, Dehradun
Date: 23rd October 2012 – 30th October 2012
Participants: 26 students of classes VI-VIII
Highlight: Children enjoyed all the activities, mostly the river rafting.

 

 

Event: Visit to Germany
Date: 7th October 2012 to 22nd October 

Highlight:

 

As part of having a seamless world, 16 LSA learners and 3 educators are visiting Germany . They are staying with host families in Tubingen and Aalen and visiting Carlo Schmid Gymnasium and Kaufmannische Schule respectively. The group is visiting mines, Baroque churches, Opera, Museums, Roman Bath apart from attending classes in school. The students are making friends and have made such a good impression of  our school that the German students have booked their flights to visit our school in December 2012 – January 2013.

Event:

 Excursion to Mukutmanipur
Date: 13th to 15th September, 2012
Participants: 45 Children from classes III and IV
Highlight:

The trip was conducted as an extension of the lesson ‘Water and its sources’ and ‘The Rich Culture and Heritage of West Bengal’. They learnt about the various ways of preserving and conserving water for the use of irrigation and agriculture in the nearby villages. The trip was informative. Children gained new experiences staying very close to nature.

 

Event:

 Trip to Goa
Date: 5th June 2012 to 9th June 2012
Participants: 18 students from classes 6 to 8
Highlight:

To discover the scenic magic, we started our journey to Goa. We boarded our flight on 5th June and after a long journey of ALMOST 6 hours, we reached our hotel.

After visiting the historical site of Goa in the form of Fort Aguada, we visited Calangute beach on 6th June and enjoyed ourselves thoroughly in the beach. Then we visited Dona Paula and were excited to ride on the water scooter and our last destination for the day was Miramar beach.

Next day we went to Splash Down Water Park and cooled ourselves from the heat of this hot and humid summer by spending time in water.

On 8th June we visited another beach named Baga Tore and also took a flavour of Goa’s lifestyle by visiting the Panjim market.

On 9th June we started our journey back to Kolkata after lunch taking back the memories of that India which is beautiful and serene.
