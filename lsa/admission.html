Admission

General Information

Span of Classes: The Academy offers admission to children in Pre – School –1 (age 3+ on 1st April of the year of admission), Pre – School –2 (age 4+) and from Classes I to XII.

Registration and Admission: Registrations open generally in August / September. Up to around 105 vacancies exists in   class Pre – School 1 and 120 in Class – 1 every year. Registrations for admission in classes I - XII open only if vacancies arise in these classes arising during the academic session. Prior applications from parents seeking admission to these classes may be sent to the school office. Enquiries regarding them could be made during February / March. 

The registration form is provided in the prospectus. Parents / Guardians are requested to fill in the registration form and submit it along with the supporting documents specified in the form. An admit card – cum – receipt indicating the date and time of the test / meeting will be issued to the applicant. This card should be brought along by the child at that time. Meetings / Interviews for admission to Pre – School-1 and Class I are generally held in September / October.

Academic Term & Vacations:

The academic term is from April to March. 
The Vacation schedule is as follows:

Summer Vacation       : 4 weeks (May – June) 
Puja/Diwali Vacation  : 3 weeks (October - November) 
Winter Break                : 10 Days ( December – January)
