Happy Hour

 

Happy Hour Events (Class Pre School I to Class II)

Event : Wealth from Waste
Date: 26th September, 2012
Participants: Children of classes PSI to Class II
Highlights: Children displayed their creativity and made wealth from waste. Some of the children made beautiful waste bins and photo frames. The learnt about the three Rs- ‘Reduce, Reuse and Recycle’.

 

Event : Kite Decoration
Date: 12th September,2012
Participants: Children of classes PSI to Class II
Highlights: They had fun writing slogans and promising to save the animals and the Mother Earth on their kites which were flying high in their classrooms. They enjoyed decorating kites adding colours to the forthcoming Vishwakarma Puja.

 

Event : Rakhi Making
Date: 01.08.2012
Participants: Students of Preschool I to II
Highlights: Children enjoyed the activity and learnt about the significance of the festival.

 

Event : Workshop on vegetable printing
Date: 20.06.2012
Participants: Students of Preschool I to II
Highlights: It was a fun filled activity. The children enjoyed playing with the paint and creating beautiful designs on their card/ handkerchief.
