CBSE EAST ZONE TABLE TENNIS COMPETITION
 
GENERAL INFORMATION

1. Tournament:    CBSE East Zone Cluster II Table Tennis Competition 2011 22nd September to 25th

                                September 2011

2. Date:                  Inauguration : 23rd September at 09:00 a.m.

                               Closing : 25th September at 4:00 p.m.

                               Reporting : 22nd September before 4:00 p.m.

3. Venue:              Lakshmipat Singhania Academy, 12B Alipore road ,Kolkata-7000027 ,West Bengal

4. Location:         Our school is located in Alipore Lalbatti near Judges Court. It is 9K.m From Howrah Station& 12

                               K.m from Sealdah station.

5. Last Date of receipt of Entries:   1st September, 2011 (No entries will be entertained after this date even for

                                                                postal delay.

6. Eligibility:        

  • The Tournament will be played in the age group of Under-14 Years (Boys, Girls); Under-16 Years (Boys, Girls); Under-19 Years (Boys, Girls).
  • The Age will be reckoned as on December 31st 2011.
  • U-14= born on or after 01.01.1998
  • U-16-born on or after 01.01.1996
  • U-19-born on or after 01.01.1993
  • All the participants must carry their valid age proof certificate and school/CBSE Board Identity Card.
  • Each team must send complete team photograph with school principal along with the entry form, signed by the Principal concerned.
  • Students studying in class – IX & X should provide CBSE registration No. given in Class-IX.
  • Students studying in class – XI & XII should provide Class-X certificate with photo issued by the board
  • Every school will be eligible to send one team in different age groups ( As notified by the CBSE) in the Zonal Championship consisting of maximum 4 players

7. Rules & Regulations:        

  • Only first position winning teams will qualify to participate in National Championship
  • No change in composition of team in Zonal Level to National Level.
  • Rest of the rules and regulations will be followed as prescribed by CBSE.
  • The organization committee reserves right to alter the venue and time of any match if required.
  • Return Reservation: Each team must book their return tickets on their own.

8. Parents will not be permitted to accompany the player/team. The escort teachers and Physical Education

    teachers accompanying the team / players should be in possession of valid identify cards with counter

    signature of the Principals.

9. Meeting: Team managers meeting will be held on 22nd September (Thursday) at 5.00pm.

10. Meal Charge: Rs. 175/- per head per day.(Arrangements will be made by the host school to provide breakfast,

                                lunch, evening tea, dinner) The amount should be paid in cash at the time of arrival in the

                                reception counter.

11. Accommodation:        

  • Teams will be accommodated in the school premises. Accommodation will be provided separately for boys and girls. No team will be allowed to stay outside the school campus.
  • Teams will be responsible for their belongings and are requested not to carry jewellery, hard cash or any valuable items.
  • Female escort must accompany girl’s team.
  • Coaches, managers (for boys, girls) must stay with the students.
  • Rs. 1000/- (Security Deposit) to be refunded at the time of relieving after claiming for damages if any.
  • The bedding charge Rs 40/- per day per head.

12. Organising Committee:        

  • Mrs. Meena Kak (Principal)
  • Mrs. Krishna Nandi ( Vice Principal)
  • Mr.Arnab Bhattacharya (HOD/PET)( overall in charge)---9831282151
  • Mr.Kallol Paul( PET)( Organizing committee-Registration, lodging )
  • Mr.Joy Chackraborty (PET)
  • Mr.Siddhart. C(PET)—Transport
  • Mr. Subroto Chackraborty-Transport
  • Mrs. Sandhya Ghosh (PET)

13. Reception:        

  • The teams coming by train will be received at Howrah Station from 10:00 AM-4:00pm 22nd September. Transport will be provided by us.( advance information should be sent to host team regarding travel plans)
  • All schools must submit school flag at the reception counter.

14. Jury of Appeal:        

  • CBSE observer.
  • Mrs. Meena Kak (Principal, LSA)
