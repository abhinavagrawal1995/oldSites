LAKSHMIPAT SINGHANIA ACADEMY

12-B, Alipore Road, Kolkata

The nine storied architectural elegance proudly proclaims a place amongst the leading elite schools of the city. In June 1996, with just about 450 students, the school came into existence. Then on, in leaps and bounds, it has made progress, both in its stature and status and now it is a 10+2, CBSE, English Medium, Co-Educational Day School with a student strength of more than 1700.

At LSA, we believe in a holistic development of personality. Integrating the traditional wisdom of our forefathers and the latest emerging trends in education, globally. We emphasise on a sizeable space in our year's calendar of activities to be allotted to co-curricular activities encompassing every field and facet of learning; viz music, art, dance, dramatics, debate, elocution, quiz, creative writing, radio jockeying, the many different sports, tours and travels, nature and the rest of it.

LSA is a school that ‘looks beyond’, technologically advanced inculcating right values and having exceptionally brilliant board results year after year. We have a long way to go and with grit and determination to constantly better our performance every time, we shall surmount every barrier.


Principal's Desk

The younger generation today is exposed to the knowledge and information boom. They are highly spirited. Their confidence and competence levels are higher. They are smarter than their predecessors and are privileged in many ways. The need of the hour for schools is therefore, to instill in them certain universal values like discipline, honesty, obedience, humility, compassion, etc.

 

However, as the Principal of Lakshmipat Singhania Academy, I emphasize on the letter ‘P’.

P for POSITIVITY
P for PERSEVERANCE
P for PERSISTENCE
P for PURITY OF PURPOSE 
P for PRINCIPLES (here it stands for ethical & moral values)
