Date: 5th September 2012

Event: Best Teachers Award Ceremony 2012

Organised By: Calcutta Municipal Corporation & Kolkata Police, Government of West Bengal

Highlight: Mr. Arnab Bhattacharya received the Best Teachers Award Ceremony 2012 for his contribution in education.

 

Date: 10th July 2012

Event: State Teacher Award for International Math Olympiad

Organized By: Science Olympiad Foundation

Highlight: Our teacher Mr. Subrato Chakraborty received the ‘State Teacher Award’ for International Math Olympiad.

 

Date: 6th July 2012

Event: Bar-To-The Medal of Merit

Venue: Raj Bhavan

Highlight: Mr. Darshan Singh Gill received the highest state level award for an adult leader, from His Excellency, Sri M. K. Narayanan, Governor of West Bengal for his contribution towards the scout movement.

Pratyush Agarwal stood State Rank 1st
