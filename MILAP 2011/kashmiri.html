FAMOUS KASHMIRI DISHES ARE:-

 

KASHMIRI BIRYANI

BHOOKARS RATINGS:-

 

Kashmiri Biryani is a filling rice dish which is usually sweeter than the hyderabadi biryani.It has a unique combination of chunks of pineapples and lots of vegetables.

WHERE TO EAT:- TANIMA POLO (PATNI TOP)

 

kashmiri MUTTON

BHOOKARS RATINGS:-

 

Rich, mildly spiced, aromatic gravy that brings out the taste that is faithful to the original, traditional Mutton . Just add chunks of boned lamb and cook and serve.

WHERE TO EAT:- RAWALA RESTAURANT (PAHALGALM)

 

kashmiri aloo dum

BHOOKARS RATINGS:-

 

Masterfully blended by the Master chefs at Kitchens of India, this curry paste recreates the Kashmiri delicacy Dum Aloo with choicest spices blended according to traditional recipes. Just add baby potatoes and whipped sweet curd to the paste and cook to relish this rich Kashmiri dish.

WHERE TO EAT:-KHAN BHAI VENDOR (SRINAGAR)
